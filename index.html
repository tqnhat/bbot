Hello, I'm Nhat.

I’m a freelance banner developer in digital advertising.

Coming from a background in programming (Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science ,University of Natural Science, Vietnam). I picked up Adobe Flash and moved more into animation, developing an interest in interactivity before I graduate. I moved into the Flash banner industry, combining animation and development skills to see campaigns through from project start to finish. As the banner world moved away from Flash, I solidified my front-end development skills and now develop and animate HTML5 online advertising banners. I like to code all my banners by hand and animate with Greensock's GSAP library because I can control the file size in hand-coded banners better than Google Web Designer or Adobe Animate.

I help clients to create new design from resources or adapt designs. Beside that I can help them to manage banners on Double Click, Adword, Adform, Sizmek, Amazon…

I’d be happy to lend a hand with your project.
